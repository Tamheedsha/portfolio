About Me

My name is Rishita Jayant, I'm a Picture Retoucher/Editor.

I am an enthusiast for perfection and brightness. I love to bring out the light from the pictures that hold much more in them as a potential. Pictures are memories for me, and I believe that when I take the picture to its maximum potential, I am making the memory stronger. I love doing this work and it has become my passion ever since...

I want to constantly grow, hone my skills and bring my my potential for my work as well. I tend to believe that experience is the ultimate teacher and I am going for it...
